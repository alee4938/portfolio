Midcentury Fallout Room

3D Modeling / Texturing / Rendering

I modeled and textured this midcentury modern style room in a fallout shelter, modeled after a set in the Fallout TV series. Many of the assets are modeled after real furniture and decor from the time period. I created the TV and computer screen textures in Photoshop and other textures were sourced from online libraries. The lighting was designed to create a mix of cozy and harsh industrial atmosphere.

Made with Autodesk Maya, Adobe Photoshop, and Arnold Renderer.
